⚠️ Known Coverage Gaps

SPX 0DTE Limited Historical Data

Background: SPX 0DTE options were introduced in May 2022, starting with 3× weekly expiration (Mon/Wed/Fri). Daily expiration began in 2024.

Impact:
  • 2020-2021: No SPX 0DTE data available (product didn't exist)
  • 2022 Jan-Apr: No SPX 0DTE data
  • 2022 May-2023: Limited to Mon/Wed/Fri only
  • 2024+: Full daily coverage (Mon-Fri)
Workaround: Use SPY 0DTE for earlier periods (SPY has longer 0DTE history).

🎓 Lessons Learned

  • Schema validation is critical: CBOE uses open_px / high_px / low_px / close_px (not open/high/low/close). Always check schema before querying.
  • Partition pruning saves money: Filtering by year / month / day reduces Athena scans by 100×. Cost went from estimated $100+ to actual $3-4.
  • Progress tracking enables resilience: Download can be resumed if interrupted. State is saved after each chunk.
  • Compression matters: Gzip reduces storage by ~80%. 1,728 files fit in ~100-150 MB instead of 500+ MB uncompressed.
  • Bulk downloads beat on-demand queries: Once downloaded, data access is instant (no Athena latency) and free (no per-query cost).
