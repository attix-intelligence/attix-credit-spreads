Method notes

  • FOMC minutes downloaded directly from federalreserve.gov (89 meetings 2015-01 → 2025-12).
  • HD score = (hawkish - dovish) / (hawkish + dovish), bounded [-1, +1].
  • Hawkish window block: any trade entered within ~7 calendar days after a meeting whose HD score ≥ threshold is dropped.
  • VIX slope filter: only enter when ^VIX3M − ^VIX ≥ threshold (term structure in contango).
  • Trade engine: compass.exp1220_standalone.run_exp1220_trades on real IronVault SPY option chains.
  • Sharpe annualisation uses trade-level √(trades/yr) — comparable to MASTERPLAN's per-trade 1.26 baseline, not the portfolio daily Sharpe.
