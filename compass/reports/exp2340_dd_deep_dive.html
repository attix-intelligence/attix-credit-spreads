3. Root cause

Vol targeting scale factor is computed on TRAIN window realised vol and applied unchanged to TEST window. When test window has regime shift (2022 bear), stale low-vol scale factor over-leverages the portfolio. 2022 pooled vol hit 38.0% vs 15% target (2.5× over budget), driving the 24.4% pooled DD.
