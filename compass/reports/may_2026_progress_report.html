6. Honest Risks & Caveats (LP-ready disclosure)

  1. Tail-regime DD breaches. EXP-3200 documents that the 12% DD claim fails under sustained grinding-drift (P=97.69% no CB / 77.06% w/ CB) and vol-explosion (P=100% / 96.13%) regimes. These are the two regimes the 3% trailing-DD circuit breaker partially but not fully mitigates. Disclosed verbatim in research/ROBUSTNESS_VALIDATION.md §5.2.
  2. Pre-2020 history not in IronVault. All walk-forward and stress work runs on 2020-2025 (1,566 trading days). 2008 GFC, 2011 Euro crisis, 2018 Volmageddon use β-propagated equity returns (EXP-2640), not real chains.
  3. MVN distributional assumption in EXP-3200. No Student-t, no jump-diffusion. True tails likely thicker than reported; vol_explosion Σ×16 is a parametric stand-in, not an empirical tail.
  4. H2 (sector GEX) reject is on a volume proxy, not true OI. IronVault open_interest is uniformly NULL across all tickers. Volume substitution biases the test toward the heaviest-traded names (SPY OTM puts swamp the gamma weighting). The H2 question remains scientifically open pending real OI data.
  5. Drag rate is modeled, not measured. 890.3 bps/yr from EXP-2570 is from the Alpaca commission-free + EXP-2470 ExecOpt stack model. Live fills (blocked by API key) will be the empirical recalibration. Material divergence is unlikely given execution-stack components are calibrated, but it is a residual unknown.
