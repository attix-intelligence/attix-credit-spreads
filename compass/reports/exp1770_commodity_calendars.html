Methodology & caveats

  • Spread definition: daily ETF return minus continuous front-month future return. Captures the structural roll yield (contango drag is negative, backwardation premium is positive).
  • Signal: 60-day cumulative spread, z-scored against its own 60-day mean/std. ±1σ entry, zero-cross exit.
  • Rule selection: per fold, pick whichever of {mean_reversion, momentum, flat} maximises training Sharpe; apply OOS.
  • Walk-forward: 504d train, 126d OOS, step 126d.
  • Data sources: Yahoo Finance daily adjusted close — ETFs (USO/UNG/GLD/SLV), continuous futures (CL=F/NG=F/GC=F/SI=F).
  • What this is NOT: a real options calendar spread. It's a futures roll-yield harvest. Implementation in production would require futures execution, not options. Use this report as a research signal — not a tradable strategy until paired with a real futures execution path.
  • Correlation vs EXP-1220: yearly only (n≤6). Directional, not statistically significant.
