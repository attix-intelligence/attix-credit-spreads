Methodology & caveats

  • VRP signal: IV² − RV²_30d (Carr-Wu 2009 variance risk premium).
  • OOS payoff: IV²_entry − RV²_forward_30d, normalised by 2·IV.
  • Walk-forward: 252-day train / 63-day OOS, step 63 days. Threshold is grid-searched on train {50,60,70,80}th percentiles of positive VRP.
  • Data sources: Yahoo daily close (SPY/QQQ/IWM/EEM); FRED CBOE vol indices (VIXCLS, VXNCLS, RVXCLS, VXEEMCLS).
  • EFA substitution: EFA has no FRED/Yahoo IV index (VXEFA was discontinued). EEM (^VXEEM) is used as the international-exposure proxy. Including EFA with a modelled IV would violate Rule Zero.
  • Correlation vs EXP-1220: yearly only (n≤6), so should be read as directional, not statistically significant.
  • Not a tradable strategy yet: P&L is in vol-point units; converting to dollar P&L requires real options fills via IronVault, which is out of scope for this research report.
