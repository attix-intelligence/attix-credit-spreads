Methodology & caveats

  • Universe: ['SPY', 'QQQ', 'XLF', 'XLI'] (requested IWM/IBIT dropped: ['IWM', 'IBIT']). IWM and IBIT carry zero IronVault contracts — Rule Zero, not fudged.
  • VoV panel: real Yahoo ^VIX, 20d log-return realised vol, 252d z-score. Thresholds (1.0, 2.0) are the canonical EXP-1970 rule.
  • Capacity: uses the real option_daily.volume and open_interest columns directly, not a model. Contracts where volume is missing are dropped, not estimated.
  • Correlation matrix: monthly returns, Pearson. All five streams come from real-data backtests of their own experiments — no synthetic proxies are stacked on top.
  • Leverage test: the $1/vol-pt vega sizing means the strategy is naturally ~2.5% monthly vol at 1.0×. At 2.0×, max DD roughly doubles and Sharpe is unchanged — so portfolio sizing should be chosen on DD budget, not Sharpe.
  • What this is NOT: a margin model. The leverage numbers are ex-execution and ex-financing. A real production run needs Reg T margin for the naked straddles.
