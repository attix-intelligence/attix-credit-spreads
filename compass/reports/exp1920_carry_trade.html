Method notes

  • FX carry signal: trailing 12m total-return minus trailing 12m price-return — that residual is the dividend distribution, which for grantor-trust FX ETFs is the interest income paid through from the underlying currency deposit.
  • Commodity carry signal: 3m TR annualised − 12m TR. Erb & Harvey 2006 style roll-yield proxy.
  • Monthly rebalance, long top-2 / short bottom-2 in each sleeve, equal weight, 50/50 blend across sleeves. Decision-to-execution lag of one month avoids look-ahead.
  • EXP-1220 daily-return series built from the same trade engine on real IronVault SPY chains.
  • All data Yahoo Finance with auto_adjust=False; no synthetic prices anywhere.
