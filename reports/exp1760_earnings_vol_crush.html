Approach

  • Earnings universe: 7 Magnificent 7 names (AAPL, MSFT, NVDA, AMZN, GOOGL, META, TSLA). 24 quarterly earnings per name × 6 years ≈ 168 possible events.
  • Trade side: SPY iron condor for AAPL/MSFT (large SPY weight), QQQ for the Nasdaq-heavy names.
  • Entry: trading day before earnings (using close prices).
  • Exit: trading day after earnings (using close prices — proxy for opening gap).
  • Structure: iron condor at 15-delta shorts / 5-delta hedges, 7-14 DTE (fallback 4-21 DTE if no short-dated available).
  • Size: 2% of capital per trade, max 5 contracts.
