5. Model Specification

Bid-Ask Spread

From IronVault: (high - low) / close for liquid contracts (volume > 50, price > $0.20). Converted to cents via option price proxy. Half-spread applied per leg, round trip.

Market Impact (Almgren-Chriss)

temporary = η × √(participation) × 10,000 (η = 0.10)
permanent = γ × participation × 10,000 (γ = 0.05)
Total = (temporary + permanent) × legs_per_trade

Fill Probability

Logistic model: P(fill) = 1 / (1 + exp(25 × (participation - 0.10)))
95% fill at 1% ADV, 50% at 10% ADV, 10% at 50% ADV. Limit orders: 85% of market order probability.

Latency Impact

Market orders: half_spread + latency_ms × 0.01 bps
Limit orders: 0.2 × spread + latency_ms × 0.005 bps
Models adverse selection cost during fast-moving markets.
