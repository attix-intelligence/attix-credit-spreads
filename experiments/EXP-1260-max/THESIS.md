# EXP-1260-max: Factor Exposure Analyzer

## Hypothesis
Decomposing credit spread returns into standard factor exposures reveals hidden directional bets. By measuring and neutralising these tilts, we can isolate pure alpha and construct a market-neutral portfolio that performs in any regime.

## Factors (ETF proxies)
1. Market (SPY) — equity beta
2. Size (IWM) — small-cap tilt
3. Value (IWD) — value factor
4. Momentum (MTUM) — trend factor
5. Low Vol (USMV) — defensive tilt
6. Quality (QUAL) — earnings quality
